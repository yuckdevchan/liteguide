# Touchscreens
[Touchscreens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Touchscreen) are display devices that can detect being touched and it sends this data to it host computer.

## Capactive
[Capacitive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capacitive_sensing) touchscreens are the most common type of touchscreen. They detect a touch by measuring the change in capacitance when a finger touches the screen. This type of touchscreen is very responsive (users report its nice to use) and supports multi-touch gestures.

## Resistive
[Resistive touchscreens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resistive_touchscreen) detect touch by measuring the change in physical resistance when a object such as someone's finger or a stylus presses down. Users commonly report that these are frustrating to use.
