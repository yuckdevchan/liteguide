# SOC Computers
SOC stands for [System on a Chip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/System_on_a_chip).

A **system on a chip** is a **single** chip that **contains all** or **most** of the **components** of a **computer**. The **primary chip** usually **includes** the [CPU](/guides/hardware/cpu), [GPU](/guides/hardware/gpu), [RAM](/guides/hardware/ram), [NIC](/guides/hardware/nic), and other components.

## Devices that use SOCs:
- Most smartphones & tablets
- Laptops with Snapdragon chips
- Macbook laptops with Apple M-Series chips
