# GPU
[GPU](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphics_processing_unit) stands for Graphics Processing Unit.

**NOTE**: The terms 'graphics card / video card' and 'GPU' are often used interchangeably. Most people agree that they are not the same thing.

## Ways to Include a GPU in a Computer
GPU chips can be directly built into, or mounted to the motherboard.

GPUs can also be integrated into the CPU. This is called an integrated GPU, as opposed to discrete GPU.

GPUs can be mounted to their own board which itself can be attached to the motherboard. The resulting board is called a [graphics card](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graphics_card).
